# 5-Minute Demo Script

1. Open the dashboard and explain the business problem in one sentence: cloud spend is growing, but the team lacks a clear action list.
2. Upload the sample CSV template and show how the preview confirms the import before analysis.
3. Highlight the monthly spend, projected savings, and optimisation score after the import runs.
4. Walk through the top three recommendations and explain why each one matters operationally.
5. Show the service breakdown and connect spend concentration to the recommendations.
6. Scroll to the customer journey and architecture sections to show that the product is designed for usability and extensibility.
7. Close by stating the assumptions, trade-offs, and next steps, especially richer CSV mapping, alerting, and team workflows.
