# Demo Video Guide — 22North (3–5 Minutes)

## Pre-recording Checklist

1. Run `npm install` then `npm run dev`
2. Open http://localhost:5173
3. Have `practice-cloud-costs.csv` ready
4. Close unrelated tabs and notifications

---

## Script (~4 minutes)

### 0:00 – 0:30 | Slide 1 — Introduction

> "Hi, I'm [Name] from [Team Name], [College Name]. We built **22North — Cloud Cost Intelligence Platform** for Challenge 5, Hackathon 2026. Our tagline: Turning Cloud Costs into Business Insights."

---

### 0:30 – 1:15 | Slide 2–3 — Problem & Solution

> "Growing SaaS companies waste 30 to 35 percent of cloud spend. 22North solves this with a simple flow: Upload, Analyze, Visualize, Save — no live cloud integrations needed."

**On screen:** Dashboard with sample data loaded.

---

### 1:15 – 2:15 | Slide 4–5 — Features & Journey

> "The platform has four capabilities: a cost dashboard, waste detection, smart recommendations, and savings projections."

**Actions:**
- Point to KPI cards (monthly spend, projected savings, optimisation score)
- Scroll to ranked recommendations
- Click filter chips: Rightsize, Cleanup, All

---

### 2:15 – 3:15 | Slide 5–6 — CSV Import Demo

> "Step one of the journey: upload a CSV billing export."

**Actions:**
- Open CSV Import panel
- Upload `practice-cloud-costs.csv`
- Show preview and column mapping
- Click **Analyse CSV**
- Show updated dashboard numbers

---

### 3:15 – 4:00 | Slide 8–9 — Screenshots & Business Value

> "For a company spending $100K per month, 22North can identify $18K in monthly savings — $216K annually."

**Actions:**
- Show service breakdown chart
- Scroll to Customer Journey and Architecture sections
- Show import history if MySQL is connected

---

### 4:00 – 4:30 | Slide 10 — Closing

> "Find our code at github.com/JaymeenDevatka/22North. Thank you — 22North, Turning Cloud Costs into Business Insights."
